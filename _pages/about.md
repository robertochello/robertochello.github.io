## Professional Focus

## Engineering Approach

I prefer solutions that are clear, modular and easy to maintain. In automation projects, this means designing logic with a strong separation between commands, feedbacks, alarms, interlocks and operating states.

## Technical Background

My academic background is in Computer Engineering with specialization in Robotics and Automation. My practical work is focused on applying those foundations to real industrial contexts, where reliability, clarity and maintainability are key requirements.
