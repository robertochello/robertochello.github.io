I am a **Computer Engineer specialized in Robotics and Automation**, currently focused on **PLC software engineering**, **industrial automation**, **motion control** and **technical documentation**.

My work is guided by a simple principle: a solution is truly good only when it is not just functional, but also **clear**, **robust** and **easy to maintain**.

## Professional Focus

My core professional interest is industrial automation, especially where software structure and engineering clarity make a real difference.  
I am particularly interested in:

- PLC software development;
- industrial control logic;
- motion control and axis-related applications;
- maintainable software architecture;
- structured technical documentation;
- project organization and engineering standards.

## Engineering Mindset

I prefer working with clean logic, explicit naming and well-structured project organization.

In practice, this means paying attention not only to what the software does, but also to **how it is built**:
- commands should be clearly separated from feedbacks;
- alarms and states should be easy to understand;
- structures should be reusable;
- documentation should help future maintenance, not just satisfy a formal requirement.

This mindset applies both to PLC projects and to the broader technical workflow around them.
