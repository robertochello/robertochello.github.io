Who I Am

Engineering with structure, clarity and maintainability

My work sits at the intersection of industrial automation and software engineering. I focus on designing PLC logic and automation-oriented solutions that are not only functional, but also clear, robust and easy to maintain over time.

I am particularly interested in structured project organization, naming standards, motion control, technical documentation and practical engineering workflows that improve both implementation and long-term support.
